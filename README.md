# 📞 **Project Title**
**Call Center Performance Analysis Dashboard**

##  **Project Background**
Call centers play a critical role in customer experience and service delivery. However, managing high volumes of calls while maintaining service quality can be challenging, especially without clear visibility into performance metrics.

This project was developed to analyze call center operations, customer behavior, and service efficiency. The goal was to transform raw call data into a visual dashboard that helps stakeholders monitor performance, identify service gaps, and improve overall customer satisfaction.


## **Project Overview**
This dashboard provides a comprehensive view of call center activities by tracking key metrics such as total customers, revenue, call volume, and service performance indicators.

It enables users to:
* Monitor customer base and revenue performance
* Analyze call distribution across customer segments
* Track dropped, blocked, and unanswered calls
* Understand customer demographics (credit rating, occupation, marital status)
* Evaluate service efficiency and engagement levels

Interactive filters allow deeper analysis based on customer type and demographics.

## **Key Insights**
### 1. Large Customer Base with Strong Revenue
* Total customers: **20,000**
* Monthly revenue: **$1.17M+**
* Indicates a stable and revenue-generating customer base

### 2. High Call Volume with Service Gaps
* Calls received: **2.3M+**
* High number of **unanswered calls (570K+)**
* Suggests capacity or responsiveness issues

### 3. Noticeable Call Failures
* Dropped calls: **120K+**
* Blocked calls: **80K+**
* Indicates potential network or system inefficiencies

### 4. Customer Distribution by Occupation
* Majority of customers fall under **“Other” (72.3%)**
* Professionals contribute a smaller but significant portion
* Highlights lack of detailed segmentation

### 5. Credit Rating Concentration

* Most customers fall within **high to good credit categories**
* Indicates relatively reliable and valuable customer segments

### 6. Active vs Total Subscribers Gap
* Active subscribers: **26,896**
* Suggests potential churn or inactive users within the customer base

## **Recommendations**
###  1. Improve Call Response Rate
* Increase staffing during peak hours
* Introduce automated response systems (IVR)
* Reduce unanswered calls significantly

### 2. Address Call Failures
* Investigate causes of dropped and blocked calls
* Optimize network infrastructure and system performance

###  3. Enhance Customer Segmentation
* Break down the large “Other” category into clearer groups
* Enables more targeted marketing and service strategies

### 4. Increase Customer Engagement
* Develop strategies to convert inactive users to active subscribers
* Use personalized communication and offers

###  5. Optimize Workforce Planning
* Align staff availability with call volume trends
* Improve efficiency and reduce service delays

###  6. Leverage High-Value Customers

* Focus on customers with strong credit ratings
* Provide premium services or loyalty incentives
